About Sync House Solutions

Sync House Solutions is a media company headquartered in Accra, Ghana, specialising in LED screen solutions for corporate bodies, music artists, religious groups, and individuals. We provide end-to-end services including consultation, sales, repairs, installation, and event support for clients across the regions of Ghana.

Established in 2019 and duly incorporated in 2023 with the Office of the Registrar of Companies, Ghana. Sync House Solutions has grown into a trusted provider of LED screen solutions within the media and events industry. Founded by Gabriel Obeng Kwakye, the company—along with its dedicated team—has collaborated with leading corporate organisations and renowned music artists, delivering reliable and innovative LED display solutions for various events.

Through our commitment to excellence and technology-driven service, Sync House Solutions continues to stand out as a distinguished name in Ghana's media landscape.
